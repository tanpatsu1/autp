# Growth Strategy

## Growth Thesis

autp should not try to grow as "another bookmark app." The first growth wedge should be:

> a private board for links you are considering, especially things you might buy.

This is concrete, easy to show, and close to behavior people already have: scattered tabs, screenshots, chat messages, notes, wishlists, and saved posts.

## Initial Audience

Primary:

- people comparing products before buying;
- fashion / brand / beauty shoppers;
- people saving furniture, gadgets, travel options, gifts, or hobby gear;
- users who repeatedly lose links in chats, tabs, notes, and SNS feeds.

Secondary:

- students and self-learners collecting resources;
- people organizing local services or life-admin links privately;
- creators or prosumers collecting brand and product references.

## Positioning

Near-term message:

- "Save the links you are considering, add notes, compare later."
- "A private board for things you might buy."
- "Turn scattered tabs into a shortlist."
- "Keep the link, the reason, and the next decision together."

Avoid:

- "AI finds the best option."
- "Never lose any link again."
- "Tracks every price automatically."
- "Recommends the best hospital or doctor."
- "Autonomously buys or posts for you."

## Growth Loops

| Loop | Near-Term Form | Later Form |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Save now, decide later | User saves candidates with notes and favorites | Reminders, status, decision history |
| Screenshot-friendly boards | Private card/list views for shopping and fashion examples | Shareable boards only after HumanGate-approved launch planning |
| Template adoption | Shopping and fashion examples in onboarding | Purpose-built templates for school, services, travel, gifts |
| AI assistance | Not a growth promise yet | Summaries, grouping, comparison briefs, tag suggestions |
| Word of mouth | "I use this to keep products I am considering" | Shared decision boards or public collections after privacy and abuse controls |

## Channel Ideas For Later

These are local planning ideas only, not posting instructions:

- short demo screenshots of shopping candidate boards;
- onboarding examples for fashion, gadgets, furniture, gifts, and travel;
- FAQ that explains privacy, local-first limitations, and future sync;
- comparison with browser bookmarks without attacking competitors;
- small private beta script after Supabase/Auth persistence works;
- template examples that users can copy after templates exist.

No SNS post, external publishing, ads, paid promotion, domain purchase, affiliate activation, or launch should happen without explicit human approval.

## Launch Readiness Before Public Growth

Before any public launch or serious growth push:

- Supabase/Auth persistence works.
- RLS is verified with owner-separation checks.
- Vercel preview is verified.
- No real env values or secrets are exposed.
- Free product solves save/find/compare without AI.
- Privacy copy is clear.
- Export/delete expectations are documented.
- Review Gate signs off.
- Human approves final public launch.

## Growth Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
| --- | --- |
| Sounds like generic bookmarks | Lead with decision and candidate language. |
| Too narrow as fashion-only | Use fashion as a template inside shopping / decision boards. |
| Sensitive hospital use harms trust | Keep hospital/life info private and later; avoid advice claims. |
| AI overclaim | Frame AI as optional assistance after evaluation. |
| Monetization too early | Prove habit and persistence before paid plans. |
| Public sharing leaks private context | Defer sharing until permissions and launch policy are ready. |
